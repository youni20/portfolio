About Me

I'm a Software Technology graduate from Linnaeus University with a strong focus on Cloud Engineering, supported by a solid foundation in DevOps practices. I specialize in architecting and delivering scalable, secure, and resilient cloud infrastructure—primarily on AWS—using cloud-native tools, automation, and infrastructure as code.

My experience spans infrastructure automation (Terraform, CloudFormation), CI/CD pipeline development, containerization and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes), and network and system architecture. I'm passionate about enabling high-performing engineering teams through reliable infrastructure, continuous delivery, and operational excellence. I thrive at the intersection of software and infrastructure—building solutions that are not only robust and scalable, but also maintainable and cost-efficient.
